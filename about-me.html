About Me

Burk Frank

If there is one thing that I have learned in life, it is - 'You need to enjoy life at it's fullest'. Don't be afraid to take a chance, even if it might make you look silly.

I am a retired educator, having taught at both the elementary and high school grade level. Photography has always been one of my passions. I believe being able to see the beauty that surrounds us and capturing it through photography helps us to appreciate the world in which we live.

I hope that you will take the time to view and enjoy the photographs that I have put on display. Thank you for visiting my website.
